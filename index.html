Mission Statement

In an age of information overload and technological advancements, it's ironic that it has become easier to neglect our civic duties. With so much information, it's no suprise many Americans feel a disconnect to the news and policies of their local politicians and candidates. Better Choice fixes the disconnect between the quantity of information and citizens by providing an interface that highlights the key values, ongoing news, and policies of political candidates.

Easy to use

Watch a short demo of our app, and see why getting to know your political candidates can be easier than calling an Uber!

Get to the point

With our easy to navigate, map-based interface, get to know your candidates for local, state, and national elections. Find the candidates that best stand for your values and have oppurtunites to discuss with your fellow citizens the warrants of their canidacy.

Features

Easy Access to Knowledge

For users, our site layout is designed to provide very easy access to your regional and national candidates.

Equal Representation for Candidates

Candidates have equal oppurtunites to post information about themselves: their policies and values. Information from all sides of the political spectrum is frequently updated for each candidate.

Have Discussions

Take quizzes to understand your political leanings, interact with other users to through candidate threads, and upvote candidates to have a more personal understanding with your candidates.
